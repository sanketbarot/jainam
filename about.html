Our Story

From a Small Dream to
Ahmedabad's Most Trusted

Jay Ambe Decorators was founded in 2003 in Ahmedabad with a simple dream — to make every celebration truly unforgettable. Starting with a passionate team and dedication to quality, we began our journey decorating events across Ahmedabad.

Over two decades, our commitment to creativity, customer satisfaction, and attention to detail helped us grow into one of Gujarat's most trusted decoration companies. Today, our team of 50+ expert designers has successfully completed 1000+ events.

From intimate home celebrations to grand weddings of 2000+ guests, from simple birthday parties to large corporate events — we handle every event with the same passion and perfection that has defined us for 21+ years.

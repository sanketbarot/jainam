OUR STORY

From a Small Dream to Mumbai's Best

PartyPlot Pro was founded in 2014 by passionate event decorator Ramesh Shah with a simple dream - to make every celebration unforgettable. Starting with a small team of 3 people and basic equipment, we began decorating small birthday parties in Mumbai.

Over the years, our dedication to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction helped us grow. Today, we are a team of 50+ expert designers and decorators who have successfully executed over 1,500 events across Maharashtra.

From intimate anniversaries to grand weddings of 2000+ guests, from simple birthday parties to elaborate corporate galas - we handle every event with the same passion and precision.

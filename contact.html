Common Questions

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry? +
We respond to all inquiries within 1 hour! You can also call or WhatsApp us directly at +91 63581 11321. We are available 24/7 for all your decoration needs.
Do you provide free consultation? +
Yes! We provide completely free consultation for all events. Our expert team will discuss your vision, budget, and requirements to create the perfect decoration plan.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance for regular events and 2-3 months for weddings. We also accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability!
Do you serve outside Ahmedabad? +
Yes! We serve across Gujarat including Gandhinagar, Surat, Vadodara, Rajkot, and other cities. Additional travel charges may apply for locations outside Ahmedabad.

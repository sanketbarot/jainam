FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our packages

Can I customize a package? +
Absolutely! We love creating custom packages tailored to your specific needs and budget. Contact us on WhatsApp or call us for a free consultation.
What is included in the package price? +
All packages include decoration materials, professional setup team, on-site coordination, and complete cleanup after the event. No hidden charges.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for regular events and 2-3 months for weddings. We accommodate urgent bookings based on availability.
How do I confirm a booking? +
Click "Get Started" on any package or contact us on WhatsApp/phone. We require a 30% advance payment to confirm your booking.
Do you serve outside Ahmedabad? +
Yes! We provide decoration services across Gujarat. Additional travel charges may apply for locations outside Ahmedabad.
What is the cancellation policy? +
Cancellations 7+ days before the event receive a full refund. 3-7 days: 50% refund. Within 48 hours: non-refundable. Rescheduling available.
